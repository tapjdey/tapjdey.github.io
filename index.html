Biography

Tapajit Dey is a Post-Doctoral Researcher at Lero, the Science Foundation Ireland Research Centre for Software at the University of Limerick (UL), working with Prof. Brian Fitzgerald on the TREES project.
He finished his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville (UTK) with Dr. Audris Mockus.
He received his Bachelors and Masters Degree (under the Dual-Degree program) from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur in Electronics and Electrcal Communication Engineering and worked in IBM for 3 years before joining his Ph.D.
His research interests comprise the topics of Open Source and InnerSource development, Empirical Software Engineering, Mining Software Repositories, and data analytics.
