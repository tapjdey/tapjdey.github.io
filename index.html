Tapajit Dey
Description

I am a passionate and self-motivated researcher enthusiastic about studying the digital traces left by the software developers during their development activities, and using that evidence for understanding the developers' culture and behavior, and also for verifying and debunking various myths and illusions about the software development process. I have experience in the end-to-end process of collecting and storing the data required for my research from online sources, cleaning and preprocessing the data, and using the processed data to create models that answer important research questions.
